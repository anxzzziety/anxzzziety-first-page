Do

1. Hike the numerous trails. The waterfall trails are my favorites.

2. Carry a camera with you. Good possibility of seeing black bear and other various animals native to the area.

3. Carry plenty of water and snacks for the longer trails. Make sure to keep those snacks in a sealed container/bag.

4. Pay attention to road signs and use pull off areas to allow faster moving vehicles to pass.

5. Carry a roll or pack of wipes/tissue with you. You never know when nature may call.

6. Carry a small knife. You can use it to dig a hole when nature calls and you are far away from any restrooms.

Do Not!!!

1. Feed the wildlife. Doing so can lead to fines/arrest. Also may cause for removal of the wild animal by either sedation or death.

2. Get anywhere near the wildlife. There is a 50 yard (45 meter) distance rule to stay away from wild animals.

3. Leave trash or waste behind.
