The Good

1. Numerous hiking trails within short driving distance from each other.

2. There are multiple waterfall trails all throughout the park.

3. You have a good chance of seeing wildlife during your drive to trail heads or while hiking the various trails.

The Bad

1. Traffic in the area can be really bad. Many people do not pay attention to road signage saying to "pull off roadways to allow faster moving vehicles to go by".

2. Some of the wildlife you can encounter, can be very dangerous (venomous snakes, bear, boar, etc.

2a. Feeding wildlife can result in fines or arrest. Some of the animals that are caught being fed, may be tranquilized and relocated.

2b. Animals found to become aggressive may even be killed by park rangers.
